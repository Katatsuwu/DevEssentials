About Us!

DevEssentials, also known as DevEss, is a company founded in 2022 by a group of senior high school IT students who studies at STI College Ortigas-Cainta, Philippines. The group is composed of Kerby Denaga, Emmanuel Visto, Luke Monte, Maximilian Cruz, Marco Dimaapi, Emman Silva, and Rod Merciadez. The group was formed because of a project in their school. They spend time together coding, eating, and gaming. Eventually, this group started to found their company, which is DevEssentials. Dev Essentials was founded with only one purpose: to provide essentials for our fellow developers. Here at DevEssentials, our products are essentially made for developers, and made by developers.
